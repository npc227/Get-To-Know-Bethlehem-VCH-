Industrial

Bethlehem’s industrial story is anchored by Bethlehem Steel, which once produced steel used in bridges and skyscrapers across the U.S.

Visit the SteelStacks campus and the Hoover-Mason Trestle walkway to see the old blast furnaces up close, with plaques and tours that explain how the city grew around this industry.

Artistic

Bethlehem’s art history appears in murals, galleries, and music festivals. Downtown walls showcase local muralists, while LUAG (Lehigh University Art Galleries) offers rotating exhibitions on campus.

Students can explore Southside murals on foot, then stop by LUAG to see how the city’s story connects to global art and design.
